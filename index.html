Our Mission

Finance learned by doing, not by watching.

LCM Investment Group gives Lander College students a hands-on environment to study markets, develop investment theses, and understand portfolio construction — all with simulated capital so the stakes are educational, not financial.

Founded in March 2026 by Benzi Guttman, the club brings together a Chief Investment Officer, Portfolio Manager, Risk Officer, Fund Controller, and three research pods covering different sectors and strategies.

Currently operating with simulated capital · Real-money management pending school approval

Learn by investing

Members research actual publicly-traded companies, write investment memos, and construct model portfolios with a defined risk framework.

Structured like a real firm

With a CIO, Portfolio Manager, Risk Officer, and research pods, LCM mirrors how professional investment funds actually operate.

Open to all levels

Whether you've never read a balance sheet or you're already tracking earnings, this club meets you where you are and teaches you the rest.

Process

How we run the fund.

01

Research Pod Coverage

Three pods — Technology & Software, Healthcare & Biotech, and Consumer & Industrials — each cover a defined segment of the market. Members develop sector expertise, screen stocks, and present their best ideas to the full group.

02

Investment Debate

Each week, pods present a thesis. The Portfolio Manager and CIO stress-test assumptions, the Risk Officer flags downside exposure, and the group votes on position sizing.

03

Portfolio Construction

Approved ideas enter the model portfolio. The Fund Controller tracks performance. Members learn to balance position conviction against portfolio-level risk and diversification.
