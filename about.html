About

Mission, structure, and educational purpose

Finance learned by doing, not by watching

LCM Investment Group is a student-run mock investment firm at Lander College for Men. We provide students a structured environment to study public markets, develop investment theses, and understand portfolio construction. All activity uses simulated capital, so the stakes are educational rather than financial.

Founded in March 2026 by Benzi Guttman, the group is organized around a Chief Investment Officer, Portfolio Manager, Chief Risk Officer, Fund Controller, and three sector-focused research pods. Faculty oversight is provided by Professor Bigel.

The group meets weekly on Thursdays at lunch. Each session includes research presentations, investment debates, and portfolio reviews. Members develop skills in fundamental analysis, financial modeling, risk assessment, and investment communication.

LCM Investment Group currently operates with simulated capital only. Any future transition to real capital would require formal university approval, faculty oversight, and compliance with applicable regulations.
