Application information for Lander College students

Interested in joining?

LCM Investment Group is open to all Lander College for Men students. No finance background is required. If you are curious about markets and willing to put in the work, we would like to hear from you.

The application consists of a short form. Members are expected to attend weekly meetings on Thursdays at lunch and contribute to their assigned research pod.

Submit an Application

What you will learn

Fundamental and technical analysis, portfolio construction, risk management, and how to present an investment thesis under scrutiny.

What we expect

Weekly attendance on Thursdays at lunch, genuine curiosity about markets, and a willingness to contribute alongside your peers.

Who should apply

Any current Lander College for Men student interested in learning about public markets, equity research, and investment analysis.
