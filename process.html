Our Process

How the simulated portfolio is managed

1

Research Pod Coverage

Three sector-focused research pods each cover a defined segment of the market: Technology & Software, Healthcare & Biotech, and Consumer & Industrials. Members develop sector expertise, screen companies, and present their best ideas to the full group.

2

Investment Debate

Each week, a pod presents an investment thesis. The Portfolio Manager and CIO evaluate assumptions, the Chief Risk Officer reviews downside exposure, and the group discusses position sizing before any simulated trade is approved.

3

Portfolio Construction

Approved ideas enter the simulated model portfolio, benchmarked against the S&P 500. The Fund Controller tracks performance. Members learn to balance conviction against portfolio-level risk and diversification.
